Executive Summary

GoldPalm Solutions is a skilled property maintenance partner serving Sarasota and nearby markets. Your current site emphasizes safety and reliability and lists high‑value services like tree removal, stump grinding, limb reductions, junk removal, lawn maintenance, land clearing, and concrete. Strategic upgrades to content architecture, on‑page SEO, and conversion paths will increase qualified leads and lower acquisition costs.

Objectives

  • Increase qualified inbound leads for core services and storm response.
  • Strengthen city/service landing pages for local SEO.
  • Improve conversion paths with forms, trust signals, and clear CTAs.

Outcomes

  • Higher Map Pack visibility for priority queries.
  • More calls and form fills from mobile traffic.
  • Evidence‑driven roadmap and reporting cadence.

This proposal reflects a synthesis of your public website structure and messaging as of August 30–September 1, 2025.

Key Findings

Strengths

  • Clear service list and “gold standard” positioning.
  • Prominent phone number and contact paths.
  • Residential and commercial coverage by area.

Gaps

  • Homepage lacks embedded web form for fast quotes.
  • Service/city pages can add FAQs, before/after, and internal links.
  • Meta descriptions and headings can be more query‑aligned.

High‑Leverage Moves

  • Dedicated landing pages for emergency storm cleanup and hurricane season prep.
  • Schema markup, localized copy, and structured internal links.
  • Sticky mobile CTA and simplified quote form.

Trust Signals

  • Show reviews, licensing, safety practices, insurance.
  • Before/after galleries for tree, concrete, and land‑clearing.
  • Service area badges: Sarasota, Lakewood Ranch, Bradenton.
